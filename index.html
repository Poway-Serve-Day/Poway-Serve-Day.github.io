What is Poway Serve Day?

Eagle Scout Project


The Eagle Scout Service Project, or simply Eagle Project, is the opportunity for a Scout to demonstrate leadership of others while performing a project for the benefit of their community. The project must benefit an organization other than the B.S.A., and it cannot be commercial in nature. Completing an Eagle Project is a requirement in order for Scouts to attain the Eagle Scout rank.

- Wikipedia
