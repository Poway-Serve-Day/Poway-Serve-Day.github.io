WHO IS THE BENEFICIARY?

The beneficiary of an Eagle Scout project is the person or organization that will benefit from the project. The beneficiary can be a community organization, school, religious institution, or other group.



The primary beneficiary of Poway Serve Day is the Non-Profit Committee of the Poway Chamber of Commerce. This esteemed organization plays a crucial role in fostering community development and supporting various charitable activities within the region. Through this project, we aim to provide substantial benefits that will enhance the committee's ability to serve the community and achieve its philanthropic goals.



"The Non-Profit Committee works to encourage and promote non-profit involvement for Chamber members, and to unite Poway-area non-profits and charities with the Chamber and its members."


-Poway Chamber of Commerce
